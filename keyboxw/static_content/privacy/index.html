Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KeyBox handles information for the KeyBox application and related services at https://k3ybox.us.

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Information We Collect

KeyBox can be used locally without creating an account. When you use the application only on your device, your vault data remains local to that device and is not submitted to the KeyBox cloud service.

If you choose to create an account for cloud synchronization, KeyBox collects the email address used for registration. KeyBox may also process encrypted synchronization data, account status information, device or client setup information needed to operate sync, and technical request metadata generated when the app communicates with the cloud backend.

How Information Is Used

KeyBox uses collected information to create and manage optional sync accounts, authenticate sync requests, deliver account-related messages, provide public key lookup and client setup features, operate the cloud synchronization service, prevent abuse, troubleshoot service issues, and maintain the reliability and security of the application.

KeyBox does not display advertisements and does not use your information for advertising.

Cloud Synchronization

Creating an account is optional and is only required if you want to use cloud synchronization. The KeyBox cloud backend uses AWS Lambda, Amazon S3, and Amazon DynamoDB to receive requests, store synchronization data, and maintain account records required for the service.

User vault data is encrypted before synchronization. The cloud service is designed to store and transfer encrypted data rather than plaintext vault contents.

Data Security

KeyBox uses encryption before synchronization to help protect user data in transit to and storage within the cloud backend. The service also relies on AWS security controls for Lambda, S3, and DynamoDB infrastructure.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, email account, and KeyBox credentials or secrets.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it through the app or your device storage. Cloud account information and encrypted sync data are retained while your sync account remains active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your optional cloud synchronization account. After a verified deletion request, KeyBox will delete or de-identify cloud account records and encrypted sync data associated with the account, except where retention is required for security, legal, or operational reasons.

Deleting a cloud account does not automatically remove local data stored on your devices. You should also remove local vault data from each device if you no longer want it stored there.

Third-Party Services

KeyBox uses Amazon Web Services, including AWS Lambda, Amazon S3, and Amazon DynamoDB, to provide optional cloud synchronization and related backend features. These services may process technical metadata needed to operate, secure, and monitor the cloud service.

Children's Privacy

KeyBox is not directed to children under 13. KeyBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through KeyBox, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Contact Information

For privacy questions, account deletion requests, or other privacy-related requests, contact KeyBox through the support channel provided by the application or the website at https://k3ybox.us.
