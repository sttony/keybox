Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KeyBox handles information for the KeyBox application and its related services.

Effective date: July 10, 2026
Last updated: July 10, 2026

Overview

KeyBox is a local-first application. You can use KeyBox on your device without creating an account or enabling cloud synchronization.

Creating an account is optional. An account is required only if you choose to use KeyBox cloud synchronization or other account-based online features.

Information We Collect

When you use KeyBox only on your device without creating an account, your vault data remains stored locally on that device and is not submitted to the KeyBox cloud synchronization service.

If you choose to create an account for cloud synchronization, KeyBox collects the email address used for registration and information needed to maintain and authenticate your account.

When cloud synchronization is enabled, KeyBox may also process:

  • Encrypted synchronization data;
  • Account status and account identifiers;
  • Client or device setup information needed to operate synchronization;
  • Technical request metadata needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service, such as request times, network information, application version, error information, and security-related logs.

How Information Is Used

KeyBox uses collected information only as reasonably necessary to:

  • Create and manage optional synchronization accounts;
  • Authenticate account and synchronization requests;
  • Deliver account-related and security-related messages;
  • Provide public-key lookup and client setup features;
  • Operate cloud synchronization;
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access;
  • Troubleshoot service issues;
  • Maintain the reliability and security of KeyBox.

KeyBox does not display advertisements and does not use personal information for advertising or behavioral profiling.

KeyBox does not sell personal information. KeyBox does not share personal information with third parties for their own advertising or marketing purposes.

Cloud Synchronization

Creating an account is optional and is required only if you choose to use cloud synchronization or other account-based online features.

KeyBox uses Amazon Web Services infrastructure, including services such as AWS Lambda, Amazon S3, and Amazon DynamoDB, to receive requests, maintain account records, and store and transfer synchronization data.

User vault data is encrypted before synchronization. The KeyBox cloud service is designed to store and transfer encrypted vault data rather than plaintext vault contents.

Data Security

KeyBox uses encryption before synchronization to help protect user vault data while it is transmitted to and stored within the cloud backend. KeyBox also relies on access controls and security features provided by its cloud infrastructure.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, email account, KeyBox credentials, passwords, recovery information, and other secrets.

Service Providers and Disclosure

KeyBox uses service providers, including Amazon Web Services, to operate, host, secure, and maintain optional cloud synchronization and related backend functionality.

These service providers may process account information, encrypted synchronization data, and technical metadata only as necessary to provide their services to KeyBox.

KeyBox may also disclose limited information when reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law or a valid legal request;
  • Protect the security and integrity of KeyBox;
  • Investigate fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access;
  • Protect the rights and safety of users or others.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it through KeyBox, remove the application data, or erase the relevant device storage.

Cloud account information and encrypted synchronization data are retained while your synchronization account remains active or for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the requested service.

Limited operational, security, backup, or legal records may be retained after account deletion when reasonably necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, investigate security incidents, or enforce agreements. Such retained information will not be used to continue providing the deleted account.

Account and Cloud Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your optional KeyBox cloud synchronization account and the cloud data associated with that account.

To submit a deletion request, email support@k3ybox.us from the email address registered with your KeyBox account. Use KeyBox Account Deletion Request as the subject.

KeyBox may ask you to verify ownership of the account before completing the request. After verification, KeyBox will delete the account record and associated encrypted synchronization data, except for limited information that must be retained for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, backup processing, or legal obligations.

Deleting your cloud account does not automatically delete vault data stored locally on your devices. You must separately remove local KeyBox data from each device on which it is stored.

Children's Privacy

KeyBox is not directed to children under 13. KeyBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through KeyBox, contact us so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

KeyBox may update this Privacy Policy when the application, cloud service, legal requirements, or data-handling practices change.

The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective or last-updated date. Material changes may also be communicated through the application or another appropriate channel.
